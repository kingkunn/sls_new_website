Accelerex STRESS FREE LOAN

This account comprises of an opening balance of N 5,000 for Individuals, and supports third party withdrawals. It gives access to E channels, access to Loan Facilities and No Minimum Balances. To operate this account, you would need a valid means of identification, a recent utility bill, 2 references and 1 recent passport photograph.

ACCELEREX Corporate Current Account

This account comprises of an opening balance of N 10,000 for Corporate Entities, and supports third party withdrawals. It gives access to E channels, access to Loan Facilities and No Minimum Balances. To operate this account, each director and signatory to the entity must provide a Valid ID (National ID, Driver’s License or International Passport, Voters Card), a recent Utility bill, 2 references, and 1 recent passport photograph. Additionally there would be a need for the provision of CAC documents and a provision of Board Resolution.

ACCELEREX Gold

This account requires a minimum opening balance of N20,000 and a minimum sitting balance of N10,000. Users can clear cheque in but cannot go for clearing. Owners of this account enjoy being able to use a third party withdrawer and enjoy free AMC. To operate this account, you would need a recent utility bill, 2 references and 1 recent passport photograph.

ACCELEREX Fixed Account

This account provides a 1.5 to 4% above normal interest rate on Fixed Deposits. Additionally, it is restricted for amounts above N1M. Using this product requires a recent passport and a valid means of identification.

ACCELEREX Save

This is the product specially is targeted towards achieving financial goals. Withdrawal is once, and it has a minimum Opening balance of N5,000. Interest rate is 2% P.A above regular saving account. Using this product would require one recent passport and a valid means of identification.

ACCELEREX Kiddies

This product has a minimum account opening requirement of N5,000 and a minimum balance of N2,000. The minimum participation period is one year, with a minimum monthly contribution of N5, 000 (N60, 000 p.a.). You can also make a lump sum deposit. It offers flexible deposit options: - monthly, quarterly, and per annum; with a minimum of three (3) withdrawals in a month. This product is ideal because it offers 2% higher than the savings account interest rate (4.2% PA) as well as the ability to pay dividend warrants and cheques of values within 2MM into the account. Additionally, the top 3 savers will get a special birthday package. Operating this account requires a valid ID (National ID, Driver’s License or International Passport, Voters Card). A recent passport photograph of both the child and the parent or guardian. (In the case of a Guardian, he or she must provide guardian consent form from the court).

ACCELEREX STRESS FREE LOAN

This product offers credit facility to government employees enrolled on IPPIS and GIFMIS. It requires the provision of Valid regulatory means of identification, the provision of Biometric Verification Number (BVN), and a duly completed application form. Applicant may also be required to provide their most recent Pay Slip, a recent passport photograph and a work ID card. It is also necessary that the applicant must be a government employee with IPPIS or GIFMIS number.

ACCELEREX SALARY ADVANCE LOAN

This product offers credit facility to public and private sector employees, who are not on IPPIS. It requires the provision of one guarantor with post-dated repayment cheques. The employee provides post-dated salary account repayment cheques to cover the entire loan tenure. If the salary account is a savings account, the customer must provide a guarantor with repayment cheques to cover for the tenure of the loan. This loan product may also require the provision of Irrevocable Standing Order from salary account.
